Detection Workflow

From RF signal to operator decision.

01

Capture

RTL-SDR-ready I/Q sample acquisition.

02

Transform

STFT conversion into 224×224 RGB spectrograms.

03

Classify

EfficientNet-B0 predicts Drone, Normal, or Jamming.

04

Respond

FastAPI records events, streams alerts, and supports reports.
